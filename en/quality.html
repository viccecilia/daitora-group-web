Safety & Quality

Learn how driver training, vehicle management, dispatch checks, GPS monitoring, legal compliance and emergency response support Daitora Group’s safety and service quality.

Operation System

The operating system behind
Daitora safety and service quality.

Daitora Group integrates driver training, vehicle management, dispatch verification, GPS monitoring, legal compliance and contingency response to deliver every journey safely and consistently.

01

Driver Training

Continuously check customer service, safety awareness, geographic knowledge, and time management. We provide training on topics such as how to speak to customers, consideration when boarding and disembarking, and awareness of confidentiality.

02

Vehicle inspection/cleaning

Before dispatch, we inspect each vehicle, clean the cabin and check the exterior to maintain a safe, presentable operating condition.

03

Dispatch/reservation confirmation

We confirm the date, flight, passenger count, luggage, destination and meeting point in advance, then assign the appropriate vehicle and driver.

04

GPS Operations Monitoring

Check vehicle operation status and respond to changes in road conditions and arrival times. We support stable operations while communicating with the field.

05

Compliance

Comply with permits, license plates, and operating rules. We make decisions with safety as our top priority, assuming legal operation.

06

Emergency/change response

We also handle flight changes, road congestion, meeting time changes, and sudden itinerary changes. Dispatch staff and the field work together to make quick and accurate adjustments.

Visible Quality

Visible peace of mind for our customers, down to the smallest detail.

Safety management is reflected in the customer experience: punctual operation, a clean cabin, professional service and calm driving. We value each detail customers can see and feel.

Punctual ServiceWe confirm the flight number, meeting time, and waiting location, and make operational decisions with plenty of time to spare.
Clean and comfortable interiorWe maintain a comfortable travel space by repeatedly cleaning the interior of the vehicle, checking the exterior, and checking the condition of the vehicle.
Professional ServiceWe value basic movements, such as how you speak, be considerate when getting on and off the train, and be attentive to your luggage.
Responding to changeDispatchers and drivers coordinate in response to flight changes, road conditions and on-site requests.
