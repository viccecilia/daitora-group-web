About Daitora

We will introduce the philosophy and business foundation of the Daitora Group through our corporate philosophy, company policy, company profile, licenses, and base information.

PHILOSOPHY

Rather than providing transportation,
Deliver peace of mind.

Daitora Co., Ltd. operates businesses related to transportation and vehicle utilization in the Kansai region, including taxi business, used car sales, and travel-related services, with a focus on hire/private transportation services.

We believe that transportation is not just a means of transportation, but an important service that supports our customers' time, safety, and trust.

DAITORA STANDARD

Safety, humility, and promptness.
Our unchanging attitude applies to all of our services.

We value our company motto of "safety, humility, and promptness."

With safety as the criterion for all decisions, we face our customers with a humble attitude and provide services that exceed expectations with quick response.

This is our unchanging stance.

DAITORA STANDARD Stay safe. Cherish people. Don't run away from problems.

MISSION / VISION / VALUE

Stay safe. Cherish people. Don't run away from problems.

Mission

Delivering peace of mind and trust through movement.

We respond to a variety of transportation needs, including airport transfers, sightseeing, business use, and inbound travel.

Vision

Becoming a comprehensive mobility group trusted by the Kansai region.

Based in Osaka and Kyoto, we aim to be a company that is needed by local communities and everyone involved.

Value

Safety, humility, promptness

With safety as our criterion for judgment, we humbly face our customers and provide services that exceed expectations with our prompt response.

OFFICE NETWORK

Operation system with multiple locations in Kansai

The Daitora Group has an operating system centered on its Osaka head office, with multiple bases in Kansai, including Kyoto, Sakai, and Minato Ward.

We provide flexible vehicle allocation through cooperation between each base, including airport transfers, chartered sightseeing tours to and from Kyoto, corporate transfers, and multiple vehicle operations for large-scale projects.
