Purchase Support

Practical vehicle selection, informed by real operations.

Daitora Group operates chauffeur, taxi and transfer vehicles every day. We therefore assess a vehicle not simply as a product, but in terms of real-world use, ease of access, luggage capacity, maintenance and usability after handover.

From personal and family use to business and passenger-transfer vehicles, we help you identify a practical choice for your needs.

01

Understand the intended use

We narrow the options around how the vehicle will actually be used, whether for commuting, family travel, passenger transfers or business.

02

Review the vehicle condition

We check the exterior, interior, driving condition, maintenance history, etc. to make sure it can be used with confidence after purchase.

03

Plan for long-term use

Our recommendations consider upkeep, access, luggage and everyday usability.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can I contact you before deciding on a model?

Yes. We first understand your intended use and preferences, then help you narrow down suitable options.

Can you advise on business vehicles?

yes. We also respond to inquiries regarding commercial vehicles, courtesy vehicles, and vehicles intended for business use.

Can foreign residents in Japan also inquire?

yes. Even if it is your first time purchasing a vehicle in Japan, we will guide you through the necessary confirmation items and process.

What should I do if I want to use a loan?

First, we will confirm the vehicle and purpose and guide you through the required documents and application process. After that, you can proceed to the auto loan pre-screening form.

Is approval or a preferred loan condition guaranteed?

Vehicle financing is subject to screening. Results and available terms vary according to the applicant’s circumstances and the finance provider’s criteria.
