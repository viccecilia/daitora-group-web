Purchase Support

Practical vehicle selection as considered by the operating company.

Daitora Group is a company that operates hire cars, taxis, and shuttle vehicles on a daily basis. For this reason, we consider vehicles not just as products, but also in terms of how they are actually used, how easy they are to ride, how easy they are to load, how easy they are to maintain, and how easy they are to use after delivery.
