Privacy policy

We will inform you about the handling of information collected when making inquiries and consultations.

1. Information to be obtained

In order to respond to inquiries, the Daitora Group may collect your name, company/organization name, telephone number, email address, preferred language, transportation details, vehicle purchase/loan consultation details, recruitment consultation details, and other information included in the body of the inquiry.

2. Purpose of use

The acquired information will be used to confirm the content of your inquiry, contact you from the person in charge, provide information regarding transportation, vehicles, and recruitment, confirm necessary confirmations for reservations and application procedures, and internal confirmations to improve service quality.

3. Third party provision

Personal information will not be provided to third parties without the consent of the individual, unless required by law or when confirmation is required from the partner to the extent necessary for procedures such as transportation, vehicle purchase, loan consultation, etc.

4. Storage and management

We will save the entrusted information to the extent necessary for the purpose of use, and strive to manage it appropriately to prevent leakage, loss, falsification, and unauthorized access. Information that is no longer needed will be deleted or disposed of appropriately in accordance with internal standards.

5. Disclosure/Correction/Deletion

If an individual requests disclosure, correction, deletion, suspension of use, etc. of personal information, we will respond in accordance with laws and regulations after confirming the identity of the individual.

6. Contact point

For inquiries regarding the handling of personal information, please contact us below.
