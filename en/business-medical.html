Medical Tourism Support

Connecting access to medical care in Japan with a well-supported stay.

Medical Tourism Support

Coordinated support from pre-visit planning through transportation and the stay in Japan.

To help international visitors access care in Japan with confidence, we provide appointment information, scheduling support with medical providers, multilingual guidance including interpretation and translation, and transportation between airports, hotels and medical facilities.

We confirm the purpose of the visit, preferred dates, party size and language, then coordinate transportation, accommodation and the stay schedule in line with the medical provider’s guidance.

Services

Medical tourism support available

01

Health Screening Support

We confirm the requested screening and day-of process, then organise the necessary procedures and schedule in line with the medical provider’s guidance.

02

Medical Information and Appointment Coordination

Using information published by medical providers, we confirm appointment requirements, necessary documents and potential dates, then support scheduling.

03

Provider Liaison and Information Sharing

We review guidance from the provider and share required preparation, meeting points and day-of contact information with the visitor.

04

Interpretation, Translation and Multilingual Guidance

We explain pre-visit requirements and information for the stay clearly in the visitor’s preferred language.

05

Transportation and Accommodation Arrangements

We coordinate transportation between airports, hotels and medical facilities together with accommodation to suit the appointment schedule, party size and luggage.

06

Stay and Sightseeing Schedule Coordination

We organise a practical itinerary from arrival and appointments through the stay, sightseeing and departure, taking the visitor’s condition and medical schedule into account.

Support Flow

From Inquiry to Appointment and Stay

01

Confirming Requirements

We confirm the requested appointment, dates, party size, language, transportation and accommodation requirements.

02

Information Review and Scheduling

Based on provider guidance, we organise appointment requirements, necessary documents and potential dates.

03

Transportation and Accommodation Arrangements

We coordinate transportation between airports, hotels and medical facilities together with accommodation around the appointment schedule.

04

Interpretation and Day-of Guidance

We share the confirmed schedule and contact details in the visitor’s preferred language to support a smooth appointment and stay.

Important Notice

Important Medical Notice

We are not a medical provider and do not perform diagnosis, treatment or any other medical procedure. Appointment eligibility, examination and treatment details, medical care and clinical decisions are determined by the medical provider visited.
