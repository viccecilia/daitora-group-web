Medical Tourism Support

Connecting access to medical care in Japan with a well-supported stay.

Medical Tourism Support

Coordinated support from pre-visit planning through transportation and the stay in Japan.

To help international visitors access care in Japan with confidence, we provide information about appointments, scheduling support with medical providers, multilingual guidance, and transportation between airports, hotels and medical facilities.

We confirm each visitor’s requirements and the provider’s guidance, then organise the necessary transportation and stay schedule.

Services

Medical tourism support available

01

Health Screening Support

We confirm the screening details and day-of process, then organise the necessary procedures and schedule.

02

Information and Scheduling for Advanced Medical Care

Using information published by medical providers, we support the checks and scheduling required for an appointment.

03

Coordination with Medical Providers

We review guidance from the provider and share the information and preparation required with the visitor.

04

Multilingual Support

We explain appointment and stay information clearly in the visitor’s preferred language.

05

Transportation Between Airports, Hotels and Medical Facilities

We coordinate transportation and routes to suit the appointment schedule, passenger count and luggage.

06

Stay Schedule Coordination

We organise a practical stay schedule from arrival and appointments through accommodation and departure.

Support Flow

From Inquiry to Appointment and Stay

01

Confirming Requirements

We confirm the requested appointment, dates, party size, language and stay plans.

02

Information and Scheduling

Based on provider guidance, we organise appointment requirements and potential dates.

03

Transportation and Stay Planning

We plan transportation between airports, hotels and medical facilities together with the stay schedule.

04

Day-of Guidance

We share the confirmed schedule and contact details to support a smooth appointment and stay.

Important Notice

Important Medical Notice

We are not a medical provider and do not perform diagnosis, treatment or any other medical procedure. All medical care and clinical decisions are the responsibility of the medical provider visited.
