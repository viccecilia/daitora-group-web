Businesses

Explore our core mobility and vehicle businesses in Kansai, together with growth services supporting international stays and digital communication.

Core Business

Three core businesses supporting mobility and vehicle use in Kansai.

Using an operating network centred on Osaka, Kyoto and Sakai and approximately 100 vehicles across the group, we support airport transfers, private tours, corporate transportation, local mobility and used car sales.

Business 01

Daitora Chauffeur & Private Transportation

Our main business is airport transfers, sightseeing charters, corporate/VIP transfers, and large-scale projects.
Business 02

Toramaru Taxi

This is a taxi business that supports transportation to stations, hotels, sightseeing, and business in the Kansai area such as Osaka, Kyoto, Sakai, and Minato wards.
Business 03

Daitora Auto / Used Car Sales

We handle used car sales, commercial vehicles, and loan consultation, and help you choose the vehicle that best suits your purpose.

Growth & Support Business

Growth services supporting international stays and digital communication.

Drawing on our experience with international visitors, multilingual communication and on-site coordination, we provide medical-visit travel support and digital promotion services.

Business 04 / Medical Tourism

Medical Tourism Support

Support includes information on health screenings and advanced medical care, appointment coordination, multilingual guidance, transportation and stay scheduling.
