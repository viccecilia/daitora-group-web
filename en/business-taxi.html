Vehicle Identity

Easy to recognise. Easy to board.
The Toramaru Taxi fleet.

Toramaru Taxi operates primarily Toyota Sienta vehicles. Their compact size and accessible doors suit city streets, residential areas and station pickups. Each vehicle carries Toramaru Taxi identification and a commercial green licence plate.

From hotels to airports, sightseeing, and business.

Operation System

Stable operations backed by the wider group.

Driver TrainingWe provide basic education on safety awareness, hospitality, consideration when getting on and off, and time awareness.
Vehicle managementWe maintain a safe in-vehicle environment through inspection, cleaning, maintenance, and confirmation of vehicle condition.
Vehicle dispatch/operation managementWe will conduct management that will lead to stable operation based on local transportation demand and road conditions.
ComplianceWe operate as a business that plays a role in local transportation, observing green license plates, permits, and operating rules.

Driver Quality

The reason you can ride with peace of mind is because of the people.

Every Toramaru Taxi driver is expected to uphold safe driving, courteous communication, thoughtful boarding assistance, cabin cleanliness and reliable timekeeping. Consistent attention to these fundamentals earns trust in the communities we serve.
