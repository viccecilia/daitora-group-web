Vehicle Identity

Easy to find and easy to ride.
TORAMARU TAXI vehicle.

Toramaru Taxi operates a fleet of vehicles, mainly Toyota Sienta. This vehicle is compact and easy to get on and off, making it easy to use around town, in residential areas, and around stations. The vehicles are equipped with TORAMARU TAXI signs and green license plates, aiming to be taxis that can be used with peace of mind in the area.

Use Cases

From hotels to airports, sightseeing, and business.

Toramaru Taxi supports a variety of transportation situations in Kansai, including transportation from stations and hotels, airport access, sightseeing, outings, and business use.

Hotel Transfer

From the hotel to your next destination.

From hotels to stations, sightseeing spots, and business meetings. We will support you so that you can move around during your stay in an easy-to-understand manner and with peace of mind.

Airport Access

Transfer to the airport is also smooth.

We support your movement in the Kansai area so that you can use it with peace of mind even on days when you have luggage or when you want to save time.

Sightseeing

Make your sightseeing and outings more comfortable.

Convenient for traveling to sightseeing spots or between walks around town. Even on hot or rainy days, you can head to your next destination in comfort.

Business

Move calmly even for important plans.

It can also be used for transportation to business meetings, offices, dinner venues, etc. We support you with time-conscious operation and a calm interior space.

Operation System

Stable operation management supported by the group.

Crew educationWe provide basic education on safety awareness, hospitality, consideration when getting on and off, and time awareness.
Vehicle managementWe maintain a safe in-vehicle environment through inspection, cleaning, maintenance, and confirmation of vehicle condition.
Vehicle dispatch/operation managementWe will conduct management that will lead to stable operation based on local transportation demand and road conditions.
Legal complianceWe operate as a business that plays a role in local transportation, observing green license plates, permits, and operating rules.

Driver Quality

The reason you can ride with peace of mind is because of the people.

At Toramaru Taxi, we value the safety awareness and customer service attitude of each and every driver. Language, consideration when getting on and off, cleanliness inside the car, and awareness of time. We believe that accumulating small basics will lead to becoming the taxi of choice in the region.
